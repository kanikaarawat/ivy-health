## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### To Adjust Animations
- Speed: Change `duration` in components
- Distance: Adjust `y` or `x` values
- Hover: Modify `scale` values

### To Change Colors
- Edit `tailwind.config.js`
- Gradients use `from-accent to-primary`
- Opacity levels: /5, /10, /20, /70, /80, /90

### To Modify Spacing
- Sections: `py-16` to `py-24`
- Gaps: `gap-8` to `gap-16`
- Padding: `p-6` to `p-8`
